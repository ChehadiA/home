Who is he?

Lionel Andrés Messi, also known as Leo Messi, is an 35 year old Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the captain the Argentina national team, and is regarded as one of the best or even the best soccer play in the history of the sport.
